## LT Process for Escalating a Customer Issue to Support Incident Coordination for resolution

1. Join the Slack [#lt-direct-escalations](https://github-grid.enterprise.slack.com/archives/C032C2W3V7T) channel 
2. Share a brief description of why the customer escalated their issue to you. Please include Zendesk ticket #. If Customer does not have one, they may be referred to https://support.github.com/
3. Please note that by default, SIC prioritises Premium Plus Urgent tickets, meaning non urgent tickets will be handled with normal priority - at weekends this may mean the ticket is handled on the folloiwing monday. If this escalation is URGENT please run .pager trigger support-incident-coordination (LT Escalation) in the [#lt-direct-escalations](https://github-grid.enterprise.slack.com/archives/C032C2W3V7T) channel 
4. If you require updates beyond acknowledgement, please add ** update me **
